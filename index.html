Our Club

Arsenal are one of the largest and best-known clubs in the world. A consequence of their largely erstwhile success is one of the most widespread diverse fan bases in all of football. No longer can North London natives snobbishly look down their noses at American, Asian or African Gooners, for whom a trip to the Emirates is not a mere fun weekend trip but a pilgrimage. Here we look at all the places from which Arsenal fans hail, and examine just how widespread love of the Gunners is.
